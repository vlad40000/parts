# Pricing Source Policy

Final normalized price fields may only be populated from:

- Encompass
- D&L Parts lookup

Forbidden final pricing sources:

- Sears PartsDirect
- PartSelect
- Parts Dr
- AppliancePartsPros
- RepairClinic
- OEM pages
- Google snippets
- AI/model output
- OCR from screenshots
- estimates
- marketplace prices

Discovery sources may expose prices in raw captured evidence, but those prices must not be promoted into normalized BOM pricing fields.
